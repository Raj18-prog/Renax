Process for test drive

Before The Test Drive:


1.Check the exterior and interior of the car for any signs of accidental damage or wear.
2.Open and close all the doors, the trunk, and the hood to make sure everything operates smoothly.
3.Adjust the seat, steering wheel, and mirrors to your preferred position.
4.Familiarise yourself with the controls.

During the Test Drive:

1.Pay attention to the car's handling, braking, and acceleration.
2.Take the car across a different spectrum of roads to get a feel for its performance in different driving conditions.
3.Test the audio and navigation system.
4.Make a note of any comfort, discomfort, and performance issues.

After the Test Drive:


1.Take some time to reflect on your experience.
2.Discuss your observations with your friends, family, and the salesperson and address any questions you may have.
3.Don't be afraid to ask to test drive the car again or to test drive a different version of the car.

Convenient Time and Place:


1.Schedule the test drive at a time that's convenient for you and make sure you get enough time for a thorough evaluation.
2.Choose a quiet, safe place for the test drive, free from distractions. Use this opportunity to interact with the car and get to know it better.

Things to Take Along:


1.A driver's licence
2.A list of questions you want to ask the salesperson
3.A friend or family member who can accompany you and offer their valuable insights
4.A smartphone to take photos of the car
5.A notebook to make notes and record your observations (if you're missing the charm of putting pen to paper)
